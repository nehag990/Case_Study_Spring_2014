Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (Learn More)

Click Here if you have already gone through the VDI request process, otherwise Click Here to request VDI.

VDI will provide you secure access to an individual desktop in the GE cloud with full access to a full suite of GE apps. You can access your data and applications at any time, from any device.

To use VDI, you need:
  • SSO
  • Pin + Token
  • AD Account
  • E2K Account
  • Billing method

To request access to VDI:



1. Copy the following link onto you clipboard:

http://supportcentral.ge.com/ProcessMaps/form_new_request.asp?prod_id=121411&form_id=475376&node_id=1399371

2. Open a GE browser found on the Published Apps screen.


3. Paste the previously copied link into your GE browser search bar. Press enter to access the VDI workflow.

To access Virtual Desktop:



1. Open the Published Apps screen that you have come accustomed to. You will now see a Launch VDI button.


2. Select the Launch VDI button to launch your VDI instance.

Mobile Device Registration (Learn More)

Click Here to GE-ize your mobile device by registering your phone.

To register your mobile device:



1. Copy the following link onto you clipboard:

https://idm.ge.com/mdp/home.jsp


2. Open a GE browser found on the Published Apps screen.


3. Paste the previously copied link into your GE browser search bar. Press enter to access the mobile device registration portal.

Register your mobile device to have access to GE Mail, Calendars, and a GE browser on your personal phone.

To register your mobile device, you need:
  • SSO
  • Mobile Device
